I am a researcher specializing in artificial intelligence applications in biomedical engineering, with a particular focus on cardiovascular health. My work encompasses advanced ECG analysis, early detection and classification of cardiac diseases, and the development of robust signal and image processing techniques. I am particularly interested in designing multimodal deep learning models for both classification and segmentation tasks, leveraging the synergy between diverse biomedical data sources to improve diagnostic accuracy. My research aims to bridge the gap between AI-driven methodologies and clinical practice, contributing to more precise, efficient, and interpretable healthcare solutions.
